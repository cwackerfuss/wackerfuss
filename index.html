Personal.

The Retrospective Version

It all started on April 18th, 1992, the day that South Africa had its 1st Test Cricket since 1970 (hey, it’s literally the only event I could find that happened on my birthday). I was born and raised in the suburbs of the Twin Cities, Minnesota and grew up with a passion for playing music and building things.

Throughout grade school, my dream was to become an architect. I would constantly draw (likely architecturally impossible) home designs in my school notebooks. I've always loved the gray area between design and technicality, and I believe my vision to become an architect was the first manifestation of that love. I never followed that dream, but my professional interests have always been guided by an innate desire to envision and build.

The Short and Sweet Version

I live in Uptown, Minneapolis, with two cats and my lovely partner, Caitlin. I enjoy playing the drums, biking, entrepreneurship, coffeeshops, and of course, coding and design.
